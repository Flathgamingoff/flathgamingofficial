Rules

Type: Survival Multiplayer (No Nether). Economy + Teams + Land Claim.

  1. No griefing or stealing. Respect builds and chests.
  2. No Nether access — nether is disabled (portals or bypasses = ban).
  3. No hacking or exploiting. No x-ray/duplication/hacks.
  4. Allowed mods: Optifine, Sodium, Minimap (no entity radar).
  5. PvP only with mutual agreement; no spawn-killing.
  6. Teams & wars allowed with consent. Use /team.
  7. Use /claim to protect land; unclaimed builds have no staff protection.
  8. Economy: Use /shop, /balance, /pay; shop exploits will be punished.
  9. Be respectful — no toxicity, harassment or NSFW content.
  10. Farms & redstone: avoid lag machines or excessive tickers.
